Scope Locks

In
  • Mini campaign for one offering · offering-agnostic template
  • ~50 target accounts (TAL + direct prospects)
  • 6-week hard stop — Webinar W3, Workshop W5, Classification W6
  • Channels: Email · LinkedIn (Organic + Paid) · Website · Tele/ISR

  • Classify outputs at end into High-Intent (attended workshop) or Nurture (registered, did not attend)

  • Net-new demand gen beyond the supplied TAL + prospect list
  • Paid search · programmatic · syndication · events
  • Industry-specific tailoring (template is generic)
  • Multi-offering bundling
  • Post-workshop delivery (Pursuit handoff lives with the AE)
  • Anything beyond the 6-week window

Intent Tracks — High-Intent vs Nurture Plays

Same campaign stages, two expressions. An account's track is not fixed until the workshop branch at W5 — until then, any account can shift tracks based on signal. Classification is only locked at W6.

High-Intent · the Pursuit list

Registered for AND attended the diagnostic workshop
  • Opportunity record created · AE owns next step
  • Scorecard + roadmap from workshop feeds the pursuit pitch
  • SE1/SE2 decks deploy · SE3 battle cards stay loaded
  • Target: close evaluation or commercial discussion inside 60 days

Nurture · the watch list

Registered for workshop but did not attend (or registered for webinar only)
  • One re-book attempt (10 bus days) · if declined → long-cycle drip
  • Quarterly signal re-check · hand-raise re-enters the campaign
  • SE5 ready reckoner monitors trigger events
  • Target: reactivate to a future mini campaign cycle

Tele / ISR Plays

The ISR / tele-calling team runs two dedicated recruitment motions: webinar (W2-W3) and workshop (W4-W5). Scripts anchor to SE5 Ready Reckoner (signals + responses) and hand off to AE+SA for High-Intent outcomes.

TE1 · Webinar Recruitment

Timing: W2 (first-pass warm) · W3 (confirm seats) · Scripts aligned to Business or Technical stakeholder
1
Open. Reference account-specific signal from SE5 Ready Reckoner. Tie to 1-2 sentence offering hook (CL1).
2
Qualify. Current-state question → confirm role fit (biz vs tech). 30-60s discovery max.
3
Pitch webinar. Position the W3 session as the shortest path to see the offering + demo in context. Send WD3 LP during call.
4
Handle objection. Pull from SE4 Pitch & Objection Handling Guide — top 5 reusable answers.
5
Confirm. Book seat. Send CL3 success story snippet as teaser. Log signal flags in CRM.
6
Day-of reminder. Morning-of tele nudge to Tier-1 registrants. Auto-reminder email covers the rest.

TE2 · Workshop Recruitment

Timing: W4 (attendees first) · W5 (recover no-shows) · Output feeds W6 classification
1
Prioritize. Attended webinar + tagged as Business/Technical champion = first dials. Registered-but-no-show second. Unknown signal third.
2
Position. Diagnostic workshop as a "2-hour, scorecard-out" 1:1 session with SA. Anchor to WD4 outline.
3
Book. Send WD5 booking LP during the call. Confirm pre-work questionnaire.
4
Handoff. AE + SA notified on booking. Opportunity created at booking (not after workshop).
5
Recover no-show. 24h post-miss call. Re-book within 10 bus days. If declined → flag Nurture.
6
Classify. At W6, feed final disposition (attended / no-show / declined) into reporting for High-Intent vs Nurture labeling.
