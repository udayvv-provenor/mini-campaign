Prospect Journey — High-Intent vs Nurture LeanRich

Same campaign stages, two expressions. Track is not fixed until the BOFU branch — until then, any account can shift tracks based on signal. Rich variant adds a TL pre-launch stage and splits BOFU into round table + 1:1.

High-Intent · the Pursuit list

Registered for AND attended the 1:1 diagnostic workshop
Attended the round table OR the 1:1 diagnostic workshop
  • Opportunity record created · AE owns next step
  • Scorecard + roadmap from BOFU session feeds the pursuit pitch
  • SE1/SE2 decks deploy · SE3 battle cards stay loaded
  • Target: close evaluation or commercial discussion inside 60 days
  • Target: round-table attendees fast-tracked to commercial in 30-45 days; 1:1 attendees in 60 days

Nurture · the watch list

Registered for workshop but did not attend (or webinar only)
Engaged TL or webinar but did not attend a BOFU session

  • Quarterly signal re-check · hand-raise re-enters the campaign
  • SE5 ready reckoner monitors trigger events
  • Target: reactivate to a future campaign cycle

Guardrails

Capacity cap — 1:1 workshop capped at 8 active sessions/month per SA-CP pair. Round table capped at 5 seats; waitlist promotes via signal score.
Opportunity-first — Opportunity record created at triage, not after the workshop.
Weekly triage review — RevOps + Marketing review dispositions weekly to prevent leakage.
Escalation path — Any hand-raise from a named Tier-1 account → same-day SA review.
Reporting re-entry — Fast-path outcomes re-join the main funnel at the matching stage with source tagged.
Content inheritance — Fast-path invitees still receive the proof bundle + prep kit.
